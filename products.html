Local Products

At Gura Camp, Kikuyu culture is celebrated through a tapestry of local treasures. Handwoven ciondos, fashioned from sturdy sisal, showcase the intricate craftsmanship. Sip porridge from calabash bowls, a timeless tradition, and relish meals cooked in soil pots rich in calcium. The camp also embraces indigenous vegetables and gourds for preservation, offering a taste of the region's culinary heritage. Don't forget to savor the Farm Fresh packed coffee, brimming with rich flavor and captivating aroma, a testament to the camp's commitment to preserving and sharing the essence of Kikuyu culture.
