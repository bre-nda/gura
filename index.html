Gura Paradise

Nestled in Othaya, Nyeri County, Kenya, the Gura River is a scenic haven offering a range of outdoor activities. Its clear waters wind through picturesque landscapes, creating a serene atmosphere for fishing, picnics, and leisurely walks. The river's banks serve as a perfect spot to observe local birdlife and relax amid nature's beauty. With its tranquil ambiance and diverse offerings, Gura River in Othaya is an inviting destination for both relaxation and outdoor exploration.
